Seep AI Sales Assistant Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2024

Seep AI Sales Assistant ("the App") is provided by Seep Tech for Shopify merchants. This policy explains how we handle information collected through the App.

Information We Collect

  • Store domain and public shop details
  • Product information needed to answer customer questions
  • Messages sent by visitors through the chat widget

No payment data or other sensitive personal information is collected.

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to operate the App, answer customer questions and suggest products on your storefront. We may also use aggregate, non-personal data to improve our services.

Data Storage

All information is stored securely in Canada. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your stored data at any time by emailing us.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact Seep Tech at infor@seep.to.
